Core Value Proposition

"Most companies your size waste $500K-$2M/year on redundant SaaS."

Proof 1: 32% of SaaS spend is wasted on unused or redundant licenses (Zylo 2025). 49% of licenses go unused at mid-market companies.

Proof 2: Companies with 100-500 employees waste an average of $915K/year. Companies with 500-1,000 employees waste an average of $1.7M/year (Vertice 2025).

Audit value: The free 15-minute ShrinkMyStack audit identifies $10K-$50K in recoverable savings before the next renewal cycle.

We built ShrinkMyStack, a free SaaS stack audit that takes 15 minutes to set up and identifies exact overlaps, unused licenses, and upcoming renewals.

No sales call. No credit card. Just upload your vendor list and get the report.
