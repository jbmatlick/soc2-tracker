Executive Summary

AI Winners generate 2.7x more revenue per employee than SaaS ($1.34M vs $492K median) and 3.5x more than DaaS ($1.34M vs $378K). The "GPU Workforce" effect means companies whose revenue scales with compute rather than headcount achieve structurally superior economics.

All pairwise differences are statistically significant (p < 0.017 Bonferroni-corrected). The Kruskal-Wallis test confirms the three cohorts have distinct RPE distributions (H = 25.52, p = 2.87 × 10-6).

Appendix
Methodology

Metrics

  • RPE = TTM Revenue ÷ Full-Time Employees
  • P/S = Market Cap ÷ TTM Revenue
  • Gross Margin = Gross Profit ÷ TTM Revenue

Data Sources

  • All financial data from Perplexity Finance API
  • finance_financials TTM, finance_company_profile, finance_quotes
  • TSM converted from TWD at ~32.5 TWD/USD
  • RELX converted from GBP at ~1.27 GBP/USD

Statistical Tests

  • Kruskal-Wallis for 3-group comparison (non-parametric)
  • Mann-Whitney U for pairwise comparisons
  • Bonferroni correction for multiple comparisons (őĪ = 0.0167)
  • Effect size: rank-biserial correlation (r)

Cohort Definitions

  • SaaS: 20 public SaaS companies
  • DaaS: 18 public data-as-a-service companies
  • AI Winners: 19 public companies whose revenue scales with compute/AI infrastructure
